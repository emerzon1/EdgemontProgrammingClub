Edgemont Programming Club

Our mission

We are the Edgemont High School Programming Club. We believe that everyone should learn how to code, and in this club we teach everyone, from people who know nothing about coding to advanced programmers. Run by Kenny Ge, we write programs, participate in competitions, and code together for fun.

Why programming club?

  • Learn an important life skill
  • Learn to problem solve
  • Meet new people
  • And have a lot of fun
